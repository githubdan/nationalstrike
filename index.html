Why Strike?

A strike is one of the most powerful tools Americans have to demand justice and fair treatment. When Americans come together and refuse to work, they demonstrate that the country cannot thrive without their labor and dedication. By striking, we say no to unfair wages, unsafe working conditions, and the exploitation of our lives for profits we rarely see. By striking we can also say no to and shutdown unjust and oppressive antidemocratic or fascist governments.

Throughout history, national strikes have brought about substantial change. They have led to the establishment of labor rights, improved wages, and safer working conditions. National strikes have delivered countries from autocratic rule and tyrannical oppression.
